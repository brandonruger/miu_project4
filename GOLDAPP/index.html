About

About The Developer

My name is Brandon Ruger, and I am attending Full Sail University's Mobile Development program. I created this app for busy people like me who love their pets, this way we do not forget to give them their medication when it's due! The photo of the dog on the home page is my Rat Terrier, Ryder!
